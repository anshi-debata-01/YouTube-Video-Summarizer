A civil service aspirant shares their career aspirations in the Indian government. They are currently interested in the railways department but express a desire to pursue a career in the Indian Foreign Service (IFS). 

Their primary motivation for choosing the IFS is the diverse and dynamic nature of the role, which provides an opportunity to act as a bridge between India and the world. This career path also allows them to gain exposure to multiple cultures and international perspectives, which aligns with their personal interests. 

Additionally, their background in a border district has heightened their awareness of the significance of maintaining strong relationships with neighboring countries, further solidifying their interest in the IFS.
